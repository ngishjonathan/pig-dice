Welcome to the pig dice game. This is a very interesting and interactive game.

Here are the game rules:

  • To start, roll a die. The goal is to accumulate as many point as possible, adding up the numbers rolled on the die.
  • The first player to score more than 100 points wins.
  • If the number is 2, 3, 4, 5 or 6, you score. You get to keep your points and can roll again.
  • As long as you don't roll number 1, you're fine. If you do, you forfeit any points accumulated during this turn.
  • If a player chooses to 'hold', their turn total is added to the score and it becomes the next player's turn to play.
